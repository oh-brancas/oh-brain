3

Implementation Tiers

Phase your system in — don't try everything at once. Start with Tier 1, add complexity only after habits are solid.

Tier 1
Start Monday (~20 min/day)
Capture Everything — single list, out of your head Allen
Physiological Sigh — instant stress reset, 30 seconds Huberman
Forced Trade-off — "What should I deprioritize?" McKeown
Shutdown Ritual — review, plan top 3, close everything Newport
Sleep Consistency — same wake time, no screens 1h before Huberman
Friday Retrospective — Eisenhower quadrant classification Covey
Habit Stacking — attach new habits to existing routines Clear
Tier 2
After 30 Days
Weekly Review — full GTD processing cycle Allen
90-Min Focus Blocks — aligned with ultradian cycles Newport + Huberman
NSDR — 10-min non-sleep deep rest for recovery Huberman
Contribution Focus — "What can I contribute?" not "What do I do?" Drucker
Identity-Based Framing — become the person, not just do the thing Clear
Emotional Bank Account — trust deposits enable boundaries Covey
Tier 3
After 90 Days (Leadership Layer)
Leverage Audit — which activities multiply output? Grove
Stakeholder Currency Mapping — what do people value? trade accordingly Cohen-Bradford
Problem Selection — right problems > right solutions HBR / Drucker
Multiplier Self-Audit — am I amplifying or diminishing my team? Wiseman
Residual Results — invest in work that keeps producing after you stop McKeown
